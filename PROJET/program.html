The 4-Month System

A sustainable model empowering individuals toward independence.

Months 1 & 2: Assessment

Adults live rent-free. Comprehensive assessments of their skills and abilities are conducted.

Months 3 & 4: Skill Building

Continued rent-free living while they start engaging in work or specialized training.

Post 4 Months: Reduced Rent

They begin paying a significantly reduced rent to transition towards financial responsibility.

Independence & Giving Back

Once ready to live independently, they return the apartment, making it available for the next person on the waiting list.
